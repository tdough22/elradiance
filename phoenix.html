The Phoenix

Elegance Forged in Flame.

The Phoenix is the embodiment of fire, reborn through form and function. Designed for those who demand more from their space, this fire feature delivers modern elegance with soul. Each piece is forged with precision, wrapped in powder-coated steel, and finished with our signature brass placard—etched, numbered, and sealed in flame.

From the first ignition, the Phoenix commands attention—not just for its sculptural presence, but for what it represents: time well spent, stories well told, and connections made around something eternal.

Included With Every Phoenix

  • Handcrafted Powder-Coated Steel Fire Pit
  • Individually Numbered Brass Placard
  • Foam-Cradled Crate Packaging — branded by fire
  • Luxury Tool Set — fire poker, artisan tongs, and dual roasting sticks
  • Lifetime Guarantee — heirloom quality, made to last
  • Early Access Waitlist — every Phoenix is made to order

Production is intentionally limited to preserve exclusivity and ensure attention to every weld, surface, and detail.
