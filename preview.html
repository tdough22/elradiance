What We Build

Elysian Radiance crafts modern luxury fire pits designed to elevate your outdoor experience. Each piece blends architectural form with functional beauty, forged from premium materials and engineered for enduring performance.

Signature Features

  • Hand-assembled in the Pacific Northwest
  • Black powder-coated steel with gold-accent hardware
  • Brass Founders Edition placards (limited run)
  • Natural gas, propane, or traditional wood-burning configurations
  • Premium unboxing experience and branded shipping crates
  • Custom-fitted all-weather covers included

Available Models

  • Phoenix – A commanding centerpiece in bold black and gold. Our launch model. Founders Edition available.
  • Ember – Hotel-grade elegance and compact luxury. Designed for commercial patios and refined residential spaces.
  • Inferno – Smaller, sculptural fire pit with striking design and high heat output. Minimal footprint, maximum effect.
  • Aurora – A sleek, open-basin fire pit with optional removable grill plate. Essential. Elevated.

Warranty

All Elysian Radiance fire pits include a 2-year limited warranty covering materials and craftsmanship under normal use. Extended commercial warranties are available upon request.
